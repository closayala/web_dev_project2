About the Chicago Bears

The Chicago Bears are a professional football team based in Chicago, Illinois.

As of October 14th, the Bears at 4-2 on the season and are currently playing much better as of the past 3 weeks and also just won their most recent game in London against the Jacksonville Jaguars. They are currently on a bye week for Week 7 of the NFL season and their next game is against Jayden Daniels and the Washington Commanders on October 27th where all eyes are on the 2 rookie quarterbacks where whoever plays better should be in the front-running for Offensive Rookie of the Year.
